=========================== FOLLOWING PSMSL DOCUMENTATION ADDED 21-Jun-2016
Data for Leava on the Wallis and Futuna Island has only recently been added to the database. There are very short periods of data for August-September 1986, May 1997 and then from May 2013 onwards.
