Tweed Entrance South is a new replacement station for Tweed Heads (id. 2314) which has  been decommissioned for safety reasons. Tweed Entrance South has not been levelled into the Tweed Heads site. Using information supplied Tweed Entrance South has been made RLR. Primary benchmark is PM182920 3.087m above the Tweed River Hydro Datum.
Sensor is a Radar.
